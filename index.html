How to Play the Nexus Game

A strategic sensemaking game where participants navigate organizational transformation by mapping patterns, replacing anti-patterns, and guiding the system toward adaptive fitness.

The Board — 3 Zones × 2 Lanes

◀
Current Reality
Where anti-patterns live. Constraints, inertia, legacy habits that block progress.
⟷
Transformation Path
Transitional space for experiments, bridges, and safe-to-fail learning.
▶
Improved Fitness
Enabling patterns and adaptive capacity. The direction of travel.
↑
Strategic Lane
Intent, Meaning, Investment Logic — why leadership cares.
↓
Operational Lane
Execution, Habits, Daily Decisions — where change actually happens.

The Hexagons

⬡
Patterns
Enabling behaviors that increase organizational fitness. Drag toward Improved Fitness.
⬡
Anti-Patterns
Constraining behaviors. Map in Current Reality, then define a pathway to replace them.
⬡
Transitions
Safe bridges between states. Place in Transformation Path and confirm when successful.
